About Soulstylz

Soulstylz is an advanced hip hop group that is comprised of skilled dancers that come from a wide variety of dance backgrounds.

Through the combination of choreography and freestyle dancers, Soulstylz looks to create unique, student-choreographed pieces that let dancers relish in a hip hop community.

We perform in shows including LG, DS, RAW, and others, and are always looking for talented dancers to join our family.

Soulstylz Around Campus

Soulstylz members teach many of the open classes held by DS every week, entertaining our community with killer moves and choreography. Soul also performs in Community Collage and other shows on campus and around Pittsburgh.
