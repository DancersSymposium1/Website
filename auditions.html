Dancer Auditions

DS Auditions will be held on Saturday, September 12.

Process Breakdown:

  • Directors introduce themselves and discuss audition process
  • Each choreographer shows a portion of their piece
  • Directors teach a short audition piece (~32 counts), combining hip-hop and contemporary styles
  • Dancers have time to practice and complete preference sheet
  • Dancers perform in groups, and are free to leave after dancing
  • Choreographers request dancers and Directors match rankings with choreographers' requests

Audition Tips

Dress comfortably in clothes and shoes (or no shoes) that you can move in. Warm up and loosen your muscles. No particular level of ability, or any past dance experience at all, is required. And don't forget to smile!

For Choreographers

All dancers who have been involved in DS for at least one semester are welcome to audition to choreograph their own piece! At choreographer auditions, you will explain the vision for their piece and show a portion of it to the Directors. The Directors will then decide whether to take the piece based on the overall wellness of the show.

For Outside Organizations

Any outside organization that wishes to perform in the DS show should email dancer@andrew.cmu.edu. They will audition during Checkpoint, which occurs in the middle of the semester.

Infra Auditions

Infra auditions occur in the first few weeks of each semester. All dancers who wish to be in Infra must also be in DS. Check back later to see when and where auditions for the Infra '15-'16 Company will be held!

Soulstylz Auditions

Soulstylz auditions occur in the first few weeks of each semester. All prospective members of Soulstylz must also be in DS. Check back later to see when and where auditions for Soulstylz will be for the fall semester!
