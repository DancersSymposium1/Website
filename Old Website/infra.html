About Infra

Infra Dance Company, originally founded as DS Company, is a student led dance company and repertoire troupe that focuses on furthering the artistic exposure of dance on Carnegie Mellon's Campus and throughout the Pittsburgh Community. The Company performs at numerous events on campus, specializing in contemporary dance, and aims to create a unique performance for each engagement.

Infra Dance Company demands it's members to be inclined in both technical and artistic aspects of dance, to participate in collaborative creation of original pieces, and above all else, to deliver engaging and committed performances on a consistent basis. Rehearsal demands are 3 hours of Company Rehearsal, and one hour of open class a week, in addition to performance time.
